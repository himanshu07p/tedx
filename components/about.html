About TEDx

TEDx brings the spirit of TED's mission of ideas worth spreading to local communities around the globe.

TEDx events are created in the spirit of TED's mission of research, discovery, exposure to different disciplines, development for individuals and organizations.

At our TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
